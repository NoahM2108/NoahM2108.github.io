YouTube Video Embedding Tutorial

Welcome, in this tutorial, you will learn the basics of how to embed a YouTube video into your website!
This will allow you to enhance your websites, making them more dynamic and interactive.

Step 1

The first step consists of opening YouTube and picking a video to embed. For this tutorial, the example video used is the launch trailer for the video game LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight.
This tutorial works for any video with the embed option enabled, so search for a video that fits your website, and then click on the video itself.

Step 2

Next, below the bottom right corner of the video, there is a bar
of interactive buttons. Click the Share button.

Step 3

After clicking the Share button, a Share pop-up box should appear.
Find and click the Embed button.

Step 4

After clicking the Embed button, an Embed pop-up box should appear. This will create the line of code needed for
embedding the video, as well as showing a sample of the video and how it should work on your website.

Step 5

In the bottom right corner of the Embed box is a Copy button. Clicking this button will
copy the line of code, letting you paste and embed it into your website.

Step 6

Finally, open your file containing your website's code. Then, just simply paste the line of code containing the video into your file, and that's it.

Great Job! You have successfully embedded a video into your website. The LEGO Batman example video is attached and viewable below.
